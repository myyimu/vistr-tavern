# Product Positioning

## One Sentence

VistrTavern is an AI ensemble narrative disturbance system where real humans anonymously enter AI character groups as anomaly sources, creating dramatic tension that normal AI self-generation struggles to produce.

## North Star

If a feature does not strengthen the dramatic tension caused by a human anomaly entering an AI ensemble, it is not a first-priority feature.

## Core Hypothesis

AI roleplay often becomes stable, obedient, or predictable. A real human can introduce intent, hesitation, contradiction, timing, and emotional risk that AI characters may misread, resist, amplify, or adapt to.

VistrTavern tests whether this human anomaly can produce better story material than AI-only generation.

## What VistrTavern Is

- A narrative disturbance system.
- A human intrusion layer for AI roleplay.
- A structured memory system for recording disturbance effects.
- A bridge between AI ensemble interaction and creative writing material.

## What VistrTavern Is Not

- Not a generic AI writing tool.
- Not a full AI chat platform.
- Not a social network.
- Not a complete world simulation game.
- Not an app-first product in the early stage.

## Product Layers

### Core Layer

- Anonymous human intrusion.
- Temporary role takeover.
- Identity uncertainty.
- AI ensemble reaction to abnormal behavior.
- Dramatic tension tracking.
- Disturbance event recording.

### Support Layer

- Time-limited control.
- Automatic AI recovery.
- Immersion mode and god mode.
- Scene management.
- Conversation logging.
- Markdown and JSON export.

### Expansion Layer

- Writing assistant workflows.
- Relationship graph.
- Timeline generation.
- Creator dashboard.
- Shared multiplayer sessions.
- LLM provider adapters.
- Community role and world packs.

## User Value

For AI RP users, VistrTavern creates a way to inject unpredictable human behavior into a stable AI group.

For writers, it turns chaotic roleplay into structured story material.

For tabletop and OC creators, it provides an experimental space where characters can be tested under unusual narrative pressure.
